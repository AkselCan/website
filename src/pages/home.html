RESEARCH

AirOU mission: design and build a heterogenous team of autonomous ground and aerial vehicles, with robust perception and safe navigation in real environments. Collaboration is our passion! Real world application is our goal: delivery, transportation system, weather forecast, search and rescue, and mapping.

Utilizing:

Machine Learning
Reinforcement Learning
Multi-agent Systems
Data Fusion
Localization and Mapping (SLAM)
Robust Control and Navigation
Autonomous Driving
Computer Vision
