Our research investigates the solution for robust and safe perception, planning, and control to build a fully autonomous, but safe and resilliant robotic system.

Our project idea is extended to multi-vehicles with the ability of sharing information between fleets of vehicles. We design and develop the state-of-the-art data fy=usion and decision making technology with the aid of intelligent communication to build a reliable and robust team of autonomous vehicles.
